My Two Years in Korea

By: Danielle Farmer

Why Korea?

Ever since I was in highschool, I've been interested in Korean mediia. I was an avid listner to Korean music and watch a lot of Korean dramas. But I got more into the cultutal aspect in college. In college I majored in International Studies with a focus on East Asia. With my major I learned topics on Korean history, economics, media, and culture. I also took two years of Korean language courses. My major also led me to study abroud in Korea. I spent two summers in Korea for study abroad and they really some of the best memories I have today.
